Raven-Symone Marries Her Long-Time Partner Miranda Maday in an Intimate Backyard Wedding

"That's So Raven" actress and former talk show co-host, Raven-Symone recently walked down the aisle with the love of her life, Miranda Maday, whom she hailed as the "Woman Who Understands" her.

Symone seems to be in a cheerful mood irrespective of the ongoing global health crisis, which has caused deaths and scares across the world.

Due to the coronavirus pandemic, events such as weddings, birthday celebrations, and others have been quietly celebrated or postponed.

Raven-Symone at A Legacy Of Changing Lives presented by the Fulfillment Fund at The Ray Dolby Ballroom on March 13, 2018. | Photo: Getty Images

The onscreen star, however, decided to tie the knot with her partner, Miranda, in a mini wedding ceremony. She later shared images and clips from the ceremony.

The newlyweds looked genuinely happy, and Symone went on to serenade her partner with sweet words. After a few uploads, she quipped:

"I got married to a woman who understands me from trigger to joy, from breakfast to midnight snack, from stage to home...I's married NOW."

Over ten thousand Instagram fans celebrated the love between both partners, and they wrote hearty comments to wish the newlyweds well on the most important day of their lives.

Miranda, a social media manager for an invite-only app for Los Angeles restaurants, also went online to share her utmost joy via several posts. She didn't write any lengthy caption, but referred to the actress as her "wife for life."
