Sophie Habboo Parents: Meet Patrique Habboo and Sarah Wigley

Sophie Habboo, a well-known British person, became well-known as an actress, model, businesswoman, and reality star. She became well-known after participating in the reality series Made in Chelsea, which aided in her rise to fame. Sophie now owns clothing and jewelry lines in addition to working for other UK businesses.

Jamie Laing, a Made in Chelsea co-star and actor, and Sophie are reportedly engaged. After gaining a lot of media attention for their romance, they recently announced their impending nuptials.

Sophie, in addition to her work on Made in Chelsea, has made a name for herself as a creator of digital content, an actress, and a businessman.

She routinely advertises for different clothing and skincare brands, and right now she serves as a brand ambassador for Eucerin in the UK and Ireland. Sophie co-hosts the popular podcasts Wednesdays We Drink Wine and NearlyWeds in addition.

Sophie was born in Leamington Spa, Warwickshire, on October 19, 1993. She has experience in academics; in 2015, she earned her degree in Media Communication and Cultural Studies from Newcastle University. Sophie and her family are now residing in London.

Sophie Habboo Parents: Meet Patrique Habboo and Sarah Wigley

They are the delighted parents of Sophie, who was born to Sarah Wigley and Patrique Habbo. Sarah, Sophie’s mother, was born in the UK in July 1965; as of April 2022, she will be 56 years old. She has held a variety of positions, including that of ambassador for the nutrition, skincare, and cosmetics company Protocol Health and Wellbeing.

After completing her schooling at Bournemouth and Poole College of Art, Sarah has further experience working as a director for a property management company. Prior employers include Wigley (UK) Limited, Flint Hall Farm Management Company Limited, Napier Associates Limited, Barefoot and Beautiful Limited, and others. In her younger years, Sarah also worked as a model.

Sophie’s father, Patrique Habbo, was born in 1961 and will turn 60 in 2021. He serves as the managing partner of Matrix Interim Management, a boutique firm that focuses on hiring the best interim leaders for businesses in the UK and other countries.

Patrique’s career began as the director of client services for a NASDAQ-listed business, where he later rose to the position of managing director of Praxis Executives on Assignment, the company’s operating subsidiary.

At Boyden Interim Management, he also held the position of managing partner. Sophie once facetiously referred to her father as a “poser” in an Instagram post. As a result of her tight bond with her parents, Sophie never misses a chance to wish them a happy birthday or on holidays like Mother’s Day.
