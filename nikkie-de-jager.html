Nikkie de Jager Career:

Nikkie de Jager became a notable Makeup artist and beauty vlogger. Nikkie attended the WE-R Agency Hair School in 2013 to hone her skills as a hairstylist. She left Pascale Tesser’s Colorful Agency to pursue a freelance hair and makeup artist career in 2014.

A line of cosmetics developed by Too Faced Cosmetics was released under her name that same year (2016).
