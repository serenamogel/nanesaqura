Chris Hemsworth shows off 70s mustache while celebrating Easter with family

Recently, Chris Hemsworth showed off a new look with 70s like mustache which got everyone thinking as he looked very funny but unimaginably cute as he was seen in a family gathering during the Easter festival.

AmoMama shares details with you on the new appearance of Chris Hemsworth who rocked a 70s moustache for the Easter holiday with his wife and friends at Byron Bay Bluesfest, as reported by Daily Mail on April 2, 2018.

The Hollywood star actor brought back a legendary moustache from the 70s and it looked very good on him.

He is known as a superhero Thor in the movie ‘Thor’ which has collected so many awards for its excellent presentation.

The 34-year-old actor and husband of Elsa Pataky left his abode and journey to the Bluesfest with his lovely wife and his very good friends.

If Easter was to be described, it was very beautiful, and colorful for the actor as he got to spend quality time with his loved ones.

There was so much crowd at the Bluesfest, but the actor did well to blend in and have a good time on the holiday.

The number of friends he had with him was so much, and they were all smiling and laughing and having a good time especially his wife Elsa.

Chris Hemsworth had on a casual and nice looking outfit made up of long sleeve black shirt and shorts to match.

Chris Hemsworth looked very young and vibrant in the pictures taken at the Bluesfest and one of the pictures taken seemed hilarious as he knelt down on one knee in front of his friends with his arms apart.
