8 Things You Didnt Know About Urfi Javed

➡ Urfi Javed Biography

Urfi Javed is a well-known face of Indian television. She is known for portraying the role of Avni in Bade Bhaiyya Ki Dulhania, Aarti in Meri Durga, and Bella in Bepannaah, and all these roles helped her to establish herself as an important part of the Indian drama. Urfi Javed was born on 15 October 1996 and she is from Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. She did her education from City Montessori School, Lucknow, and completed her graduation in mass communication from Amity University Lucknow. In 2018, she played Kamini Joshi in SAB TV’s Saat Phero Ki Hera Pherie, Bella Kapoor in Colors TV’s Bepannaah, Piyali in Star Bharat’s Jiji Maa, etc and polished her acting skills somehow more. In 2020, she joined the most popular series, Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai as Shivani Bhatia. Next, she played Tanisha Chakraborty in Kasautii Zindagii Kay. Here we are telling you 8 interesting facts about her amazing and hard-working personality that you will be amazed to know. So let us start!
