Who is Patrick Sharp?

Patrick Sharp, a former professional ice hockey player from Canada, enjoyed a fruitful 15-year career in the National Hockey League (NHL), where he represented the Philadelphia Flyers, Chicago Blackhawks, and Dallas Stars.

Sharp joined NBC Sports as a studio analyst when his playing career ended, and he currently offers color commentary for Blackhawks broadcasts on NBC Sports Chicago.

He was also a member of the University of Vermont’s coaching staff in 2021.

Chris, his older brother, is currently a resident of Calgary, Alberta.

Sharp moved about a lot as a child, eventually landing in Burlington, Vermont, where he attended the University of Vermont, after residing in Calgary and Thunder Bay.
