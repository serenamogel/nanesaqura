Goh's Exeggutor (Japanese: ゴウナッシー Go's Nassy) is the twenty-sixth Pokémon that Goh caught in the Kanto region, and his thirty-ninth overall.

In the anime

As an Exeggcute

Exeggutor debuted as an Exeggcute in Caring for a Mystery!, where Goh caught it while he and Ash came to a local park at Vermilion City searching for a Pokémon Egg. When Goh saw Exeggcute for the first time he thought that it was an egg, but when he realized that it was a Pokémon, he caught it instantly.

In That New Old Gang of Mine!, Yamper found a Leaf Stone which Goh used to evolve it into Exeggutor. It was later seen alongside it's Alolan counterpart.

Personality and characteristics

Exeggutor and Goh

Not much was known about Exeggutor's personality.

In the games

Pokémon GO

In an event based on Pokémon Journeys: The Series from November 6 to November 12, 2020, in Pokémon GO, several Pokémon based on Goh's catches appeared in the wild, including Exeggcute.[1]
