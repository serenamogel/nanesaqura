Buffy Sainte-Marie Age, Songs and Albums, Still Alive,

Buffy Sainte-Marie is a Canadian-American singer-songwriter, musician, composer, and visual artist

Buffy Sainte-Marie Age

How old is Buffy Sainte-Marie? Buffy Sainte-Marie is 82 years old. Sainte-Marie was born in 1941 on the Piapot 75 reserve in the Qu’Appelle Valley, Saskatchewan, Canada

Buffy Sainte-Marie Songs and Albums

How many songs and albums have Buffy Sainte-Marie released? Buffy Sainte-Marie’s discography includes 17 albums.

Among her most popular songs are “Universal Soldier”, “Cod’ine”, “Until It’s Time for You to Go”, “Take My Hand for a While”, “Now That the Buffalo’s Gone”, and her versions of Mickey Newbury’s “Mister Can’t You See” and Joni Mitchell’s “The Circle Game”.

Her songs have been recorded by many artists including Donovan, Joe Cocker, Jennifer Warnes, Janis Joplin, Elvis Presley, and Glen Campbell.

Buffy Sainte-Marie Still Alive

Is Buffy Sainte-Mariestill alive? As of this writing on October 27 2024, Buffy Sainte-Marie is alive. However, Saint-Marie announced her retirement from live performances on August 3, 2023, citing health problems.
