First Day Out Lyrics

First Day Out is a popular rap song by Tee Grizzley, an American rapper and songwriter from Detroit, Michigan.

The song was released in 2017 and quickly gained popularity due to its gritty lyrics and hard-hitting beat.

It tells the story of Tee Grizzley’s experience being released from prison after serving time for multiple charges.

LyricsThe song tells the story of Tee Grizzley’s experience being released from prison after serving time for multiple charges. The lyrics are gritty and autobiographical, with Tee Grizzley rapping about his time in prison, his criminal history, and his determination to succeed despite the odds.
Impact“First Day Out” helped launch Tee Grizzley’s career, and is widely considered one of his best songs. The track has been remixed by numerous other rappers and has been referenced in popular culture, including on the TV show “Atlanta.” The song’s success also helped solidify the Detroit hip-hop scene, which has been gaining more attention in recent years.
