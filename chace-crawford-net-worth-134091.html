Chace Crawford Net Worth

Chace Crawford is an American actor born on July 18, 1985 in Lubbock, TX. He is best known for his role as Nate Archibald on the CW series Gossip Girl. Crawford has also appeared in films such as The Covenant, Twelve, and What to Expect When You're Expecting.
