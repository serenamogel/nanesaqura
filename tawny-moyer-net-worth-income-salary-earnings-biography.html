Tawny Moyer Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Tawny Moyer (born 1957) is an American actress who has appeared in a number of films and television shows. Halloween II, in which she played the character of Jill Franco, the nurse, is her most well-known performance. California Suite was her first feature picture appearance, and she is married to actor Patrick St. Esprit, with whom she has two children. Moyer has featured in films such as Looker (1981), A Fine Mess (1986), House of the Rising Sun (1987), and Thank Heaven (2001). Her most recent film performance was in the 2001 picture House of the Rising Sun. Molly Moyer has made many guest appearances on television programmes; she featured in the pilot episode of Hunter and appeared on shows such as Barnaby Jones, The A-Team (with Tom Cruise), Knight Rider (with John Travolta), The Nanny, and The Drew Carey Show.
