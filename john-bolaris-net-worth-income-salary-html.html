John Bolaris is an American meteorologist who was born on June 27, 195Bolaris earned four Mid-Atlantic Emmy Awards, including three for Best Weathercaster and one for Best Entertainment Host for Time Out, a television programme he co-hosted on WCAU in Philadelphia for five years. From 1982 until 1987, he worked as a syndicated meteorologist. He worked at WCBS in New York from 1987 to 1990 after a brief stint at News 12 Long Island. He earned popularity while working at WCAU in Philadelphia from 1990 to 2003 for forecasting a 2001 “Storm of the Century” that was much less severe than his forecasts. From 2003 until 2007, he returned to WCBS. He was the head meteorologist for WTXF-TV in Philadelphia from 2008 until 2011.
