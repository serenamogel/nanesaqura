Details About Matt Rife’s Career

As a comedian, Rife is most known for his independently produced comedy specials, Matthew Steven Rife (2023), Walking Red Flag (2023), and Only Fans (2021).

In 2019, he made an appearance on Bring the Funny as well. He advanced to the Semi-Final Showcase but was eliminated in the eighth week.

As an actor, he is well-known for his appearances as Logan in the sitcom Fresh Off the Boat, Brandon Bliss in Brooklyn Nine-Nine, and Wild ‘n Out, a sketch comedy and improv game show.

In 2017, he returned to host Total Request Live for a limited period and appeared on MTV’s The Challenge: Champs vs. Stars.

Rife is also a paranormal investigator who may be seen on the YouTube channel “Overnight.” In December 2022, Rife signed a deal with the Creative Artists Agency (CAA).

He released Matthew Steven Rife, his self-titled special, on Valentine’s Day in April 2023. He followed up with another program, Walking Red Flag, in June 2023, which focused entirely on crowdsourcing.
