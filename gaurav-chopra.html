Gaurav Chopra is a well-known face of the Indian TV industry as he has worked in many top-ranked TV serials for which he got much appreciation from everyone. He is best known for the role of Raghuvendra Pratap Rathore played in the serial “Uttaran”. Later, he then appeared as a contestant on the reality TV show named “Bigg Boss-10”. Also, he has acted in the Oscar award-winning movie “Blood Diamond”. However, he has done various TV projects for which he got huge applause. One of the sources revealed that he is a graduate of the National Institute of Fashion Technology in the year 2000.

Career

In 2002, Gaurav Chopra made his acting debut with a small screen and appeared in the most-watched horror serial “Shhhh….Koi Hai”. In 2003, he then made his Tamil debut movie “Ottran” while he then made an appearance in the TV serial “Sara Aakash” (Hindi) which was aired in the year 2004 on Star Plus. In 2006, he appeared in “Karma” which was aired on Star Plus for which he got much praise. Later, he then appeared in many reality TV shows including “Zara Nachke Dikha” and others. In 2006, Gaurav Chopra made his Hollywood debut with an Oscar winning award “Blood Diamond”. In 2016, he also appeared in the Georgian edition of the International dance reality show “Dancing With Stars” and also selected as a cultural ambassador. In the same year, he then appeared as a contestant on the reality TV show named “Bigg Boss-10”. In 2018, he also appeared in the web-series named “Fourplay”. Meanwhile, Gaurav Chopra has done various TV projects and appeared in more than 23 TV serials, 06 reality TV shows, 02 movies, 03 action movies, and 2 web-series. It’s like a big win that he has acted in such a large number of projects and fortunately, he got much praise and appreciation.

Achievements
