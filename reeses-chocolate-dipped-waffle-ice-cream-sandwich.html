Reeseâ€™s Chocolate-Dipped Waffle Ice Cream Sandwich

Reeseâ€™s Peanut Butter Cups and vanilla ice cream are sandwiched between two chocolate-dipped Eggo mini waffles for the ultimate ice cream sandwich!

Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 10 minutes minutes Servings 4 Calories 500kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 8 Eggo Homestyle Minis waffles
  • 4 oz Chocolate CandiQuik
  • 4 Reese's Peanut Butter Cups
  • 4 scoops Breyer's Vanilla or Reese's Ice Cream

Instructions

  • Prepare waffles according to packing instructions and let cool.

  • Melt chocolate coating according to package instructions and dip waffles into melted chocolate. Set on wax paper to cool and harden.

  • Spread softened ice cream on a lined baking sheet and place in freezer to firm up.

  • Remove baking sheet and use a cookie cutter to cut out a circle that is the same size as the waffles and Reese's candy.

  • Assemble the ice cream sandwiches and enjoy!
