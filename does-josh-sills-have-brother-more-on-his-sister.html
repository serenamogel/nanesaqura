Does Josh Sills Have Brother? More On His Sister Nicole

Does Josh Sills have a brother? He was charged with kidnapping and rape less than two weeks before the team’s Super Bowl game.

Josh Sills is a football offensive guard from the United States. 

He currently plays for the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League. Similarly, he participated in collegiate football at Oklahoma State and West Virginia.

Sills made his NFL Draft announcement on January 5, 2022. He was not picked in the draft, but the Philadelphia Eagles eventually signed him as a free agent.

Additionally, he was an undrafted 2022 player to make the Eagles’ final roster, along with Josh Jobe and Reed Blankenship.

Sills participated in just one game as a rookie, taking part in several special teams plays against the Arizona Cardinals in week five.

Does Josh Sills Have Brother?

Josh Sills have never discussed anything about his brother. So, it is reliable to say that he does not have a brother, but he has a sister Nicole Johnson.

Sills was raised in Sarahsville, Ohio, by caring and loving parents. He was a two-year starter for Meadowbrook High School in Byesville.

He committed to attend West Virginia University and redshirted his first year there.

Josh participated in 13 games as a freshman in 2017 and started 10 of them, five at left guard & the other five at right guard.

After then, he participated in 681 snaps and had the most knockdown blocks on the squad, with 13.

In 2018, Sills started 12 games, participated in 970 plays, and was later selected second-team All-Big 12 Conference. In 2019, he played in two games and made two starts.

Not to mention, he was elected team captain as a senior and led Oklahoma State in knockdown blocks and pancake blocks.

Additionally, he was nominated for the Big 12 Offensive Lineman of the Year award but was unsuccessful in winning it.

More On Josh Sills Sister Nicole Johnson​​ And Parents John and Kim

Josh Sills was raised with his sister Nicole Johnson in Sarahsville, Ohio. His sister and parents, John and Kim, are very supportive and caring. 

Josh’s parents disclosed to The Oklahoman in 2021 that they drove their 2013 Nissan Altima more than 232,000 miles to attend all of Josh’s football games. He called his parents’ commitment to going to his game huge.

Josh stated Kim, an X-ray technician, took on a second job to cover her travel costs. She changed her plans, and John, who remanufactures diesel engines, did the same.

According to Sills, “he thinks the main thing that has helped him is he enjoys hard work.” His dad and parents taught him that from an early age.

Although his parents didn’t always have the best or friendliest items, they always ensured that he and his sister had everything they needed.

Josh Sills Charges: What Did He Do?

Josh Sills, a Philadelphia Eagles offensive line member, has been charged with rape and kidnapping less than two weeks before the team plays in Super Bowl LVII.

On February 1, 2023, rape allegations against him were brought. The incident occurred in December 2019 while he was still a student and a member of the West Virginia Mountaineers.

The indictment claims that Sills held a woman against her will and engaged in nonconsensual sexual conduct.

According to the statement, “the crime was reported, and the Guernsey County Sheriff’s Office conducted a thorough investigation.”
