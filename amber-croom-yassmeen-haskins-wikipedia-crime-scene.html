More About Amber Croom

Initially pursuing a path in engineering as a student at the University of New Orleans, Amber Croom made a pivotal decision to embark on a new career in pastry.

The catalyst for this significant shift was the life-altering Hurricane Katrina disaster that struck in August 2005.

Faced with the aftermath of the catastrophe, Croom took a reflective pause and reevaluated her accurate sources of joy and fulfillment.

Ultimately, she decided to pursue her passion for pastry by enrolling in Culinard’s esteemed pastry program.

Growing up, Croom was fortunate to have a mother who possessed exceptional culinary skills, profoundly influencing her culinary journey.

Croom and Shawne Bryan, a runner-up on the “Holiday Baking Championship,” initially aspired to become architects before charting new paths in the culinary world.

Amber Croom’s career transition represents a testament to her unwavering passion and determination. She has found her true calling in pastry by embracing her love for cooking and baking.
