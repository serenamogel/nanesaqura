Biography

Austin Dillon was born in Lewisville, NC on April 27, 1990. Stock race car driver and grandson of Richard Childress. He made his NASCAR debut in 2008; he won NASCAR Rookie of the Year in the Truck Series in 2010 and the Truck Series championship in 2011. In 2011, he began driving the #3 car at the NASCAR Nationwide Series, the number previously made famous by the great Dale Earnhardt. He attended High Point University.
